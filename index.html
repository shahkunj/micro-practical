Ever wondered what it’s like to be part of the Logi-technic crew?

Let me give you the inside scoop. As a member of our team, you’ll find yourself immersed in a variety of projects, each offering unique challenges and opportunities for growth.

So, why do clients turn to outsourcing?

Picture this: you might be called in to lend expertise on short-term projects, filling in when a colleague falls ill or when there’s a sudden surge in workload. Or perhaps you’ll be part of a team tasked with launching a new project, bringing your specialized skills to the table to ensure its success.

Clients also seek our support when they need to fill skill gaps that they can’t find internally, or when they want to add a fresh perspective to their technical teams.

Salary package and extralegal benefits
Competitive Hourly Wage

We offer an attractive hourly rate.

Meal Vouchers

Shift Bonus

Recognizing your hard work, we offer extra allowance for shifts worked.

Mobility Allowance

Receive a mobility allowance for the time spent travelling to and form work.

Team-Building Activities

Join us for fun team activities and enjoy our cozy corners, where you can relax and bond with your colleagues.

Overtime and Compensatory Time

We ensure fair compensation for extra hours worked and provide the option to take time off in lieu.

Professional Development

Take advantage of our Academy program, designed to help you continuously learn and enhance your skills.

Company Phone

With our ‘Bring your own device’ policy, pick the smartphone that suits you best with a predefined budget we provide.

Attitude bonus

Our attitude or quality bonus rewards your dedication in creating a positive work environment.

You also enjoy these benefits

Company Car

You receive a company car for commuting, which you can take home and use to get to your work. But that's not all! For a small personal contribution, you can use the car for personal travels. You can choose a vehicle with 5 seats, allowing family or friends to ride along. Plus, the fuel card can be utilized for personal use (within agreed-upon limits).

Leave Benefits

Enjoy the flexibility of scheduling 20 days of leave as per your needs, along with an extra day of seniority leave for every five years of service.

Extralegal Retirement Plan

As your employer, we save money into your retirement plan via the Metal Pension Fund, which you can benefit from once you retire.

Year-End Bonus and Holiday Pay

The year-end bonus is an additional amount you receive at the end of the year on top of your regular salary. The holiday pay is an additional amount of money you receive in May.

Health Insurance Coverage

Your well-being matters to us, our hospitalization insurance covers medical expenses related to hospitalization, serious illnesses, and other medical care.

Our Recruitment Process Step by Step

  1. 1. Introduction

    Once we receive your application or contact you via our database, we’ll reach out to schedule an initial introduction. This serves as our first opportunity to get acquainted and discuss your background and interests.


  2. 2. Technical Assessment

    During the technical assessment, we’ll delve into your experience and expertise. We’ll review your technical skills and qualifications, as well as discuss available projects in your preferred region.


  3. 3. Salary Proposal

    Following a successful introduction and technical assessment, you’ll receive a personalized salary proposal. This offer is tailored to reflect your qualifications and the key elements of the role.
